Regulatory Path

Building outside a bank simplifies the regulatory landscape significantly.

✓ Required

  • Ministry of Commerce — CR with "precious metals trading" activity
  • SASO — hallmarking & purity compliance
  • SAMA PSP — if holding customer funds / processing payments
  • AML/KYC — identity verification mandatory

💡 Key Advantage

As a standalone platform (not a bank product), we avoid SAMA's banking product regulation entirely. No clause 7.2.2 approval needed.

🔔 Good to Know

  • 0% VAT on investment gold (GCC VAT rules)
  • No purchase limits by default
  • Shariah compliance required for Saudi market
  • Penalty for unlicensed: up to SAR 90K + 6 months

If We Go Innovative (Direction A)

Gold savings = likely needs SAMA payment license (holding funds). Gold-backed lending = CMA territory. Tokenization = unclear. Start with e-commerce, add regulated features with licenses.
