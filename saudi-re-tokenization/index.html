Market Pain Points

Where the ecosystem is breaking — and where opportunity lives

💧 Liquidity

Tokens mostly trade within issuance platform only — no cross-platform market.

📋 Regulatory Fragmentation

72% of institutional investors cite regulatory uncertainty as top barrier.

🤝 Trust Gap

Few large-scale success stories to build retail investor confidence.

📊 Valuation Opacity

No standardized on-chain valuation methodology across platforms.

📚 Investor Education

Retail investors don't understand tokenization — adoption is bottlenecked.

🔐 KYC/AML Friction

Cross-border compliance not standardized — each platform builds its own.

🏗️ Property Mgmt Gap

No bridge between the token layer and actual property operations.
